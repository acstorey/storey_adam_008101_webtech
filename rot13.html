ROT-13

=Rot13 or other known aa Rot-13 (short for rotate 13) is a simple letter substitution encryption scheme. It works by replacing the current english letters in a message with those that are 13 positions ahead in the alphabet. E.g, the letter A is replaced by N, and B by O, C by P, and many more. Numbers and punctuation are not at all encoded.
